Group Chunkman Rules:

Chunk Requirements

•

All best-in-slot gear is to be unlocked before rolling a new chunk.

•

Highest possible usage must be met, e.g. Yew Shieldbow vs Yew Shortbow.

•

All monster specific drops must be obtained from each creature in the area. Herbs, general seeds, charms, and coins are exempt due to being non-specific.

Special Rules

•

For each skilling pet received, a token is earned that allows for delaying a single chunk task for 5 chunks. If this delay would prevent other tasks from being completed, they are also delayed the same amount of time.

•

Each player is allowed a single reroll of a chunk across the account.

•

Teleports with a consistent source allow for unlocking that chunk if chosen, after which is a permanent rollable chunk. (e.g. Ring of Duelling adds Castle Wars, Emir's Arena, etc. as rollable.)

Sharing

•

If one player completes their chunk they do not need to wait on the other person to finish theirs.

•

If a training method is unlocked by one player, the other player doesn't require that method for their chunks.

•

Items may be freely traded between the players as desired to assist with goals.

•

The assist system may be used to meet chunk requirements for any associated tasks.

Exceptions

•

Dailies/Time-gated activities become passive tasks. (e.g. Farming, Minigames, Distractions and Diversions.)

•

Boss Chunks cannot roll as the initial chunk.

•

Closest Ironman to Lumbridge completes Chef’s Assistant to unlock group storage.

•

Primary training methods are restricted to at least 1/16 drop rates.

•

Herb cleaning is not a primary method.
